How It Works

From USDCx deposit to gold yield in three steps

01

Deposit USDCx

Connect your Xverse wallet and deposit USDCx into the Clarity vault on Stacks mainnet. You receive vGLD receipt tokens representing your share.

02

Gold Carry Strategy

Your USDCx buys spot PAXG on Coinbase. The vault executes a carry strategy to capture gold contango yield. Proof-of-reserve is published on-chain.

03

Earn stSTX Yield

Yield is converted to stSTX or STX and distributed to vGLD holders weekly. Redeem anytime by burning your vGLD tokens.
